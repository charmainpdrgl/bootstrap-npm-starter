Remove Leftover Candle Wax

Step 1
For 2 hours, place your candle in the freezer. The cold air will shrink the wax therefore separating the edge of the wax from the jar.

Step 2
Use a butterknife to further separate the wax from the jar. The wax should come right off in onw huge chunk.

Step 3
If the jar's sticker label is being stubborn, submerge the jar in hot water and wait around 10 minutes. The sticker should be easily scraped off after.

Step 4
If there's still excess wax residue at the bottom of the jar, place it in the microwave for about 30 seconds. Wipe away with a napkin then wash the jar with dish soap and water. Dry it and it should be good to use as container for smaller items such as pens, hair ties, cotton tips, coins, buttons, etc.
