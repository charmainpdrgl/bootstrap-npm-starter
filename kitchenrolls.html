Reusable Kitchen Rolls - Tutorial Blog

Materials Needed

  • Old towel
  • Any old fabric
  • Rotary cutter & cutting mat OR scissors
  • Ruler
  • Straight pins
  • Sewing machine (but you can definitely just hand-sew this)

Step 1 Cut out both towel and fabric into squares - must have the same size (choose how big or how small you want the size to be).
Step 2 Lay the towel outer side facing up and place the fabric on top with the inner side facing up. Pin the sides together. You can mark the edge where to start and stop sewing (this will allow you to turn it inside out).
Step 3 Now you can start sewing around the edges. Start with a few backstitches to secure the thread. Remember where to stop sewing so you don't completely close it up.
Step 4 Turn it inside out. Don't forget to push out the corners so it's not folded. Sew the gap to close it up. Now, sew diagonally from corner to corner to stop the towel from getting misshapen and baggy when washed.
