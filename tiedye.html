Tie Dye - Tutorial Blog

Materials Needed

  • Bleach or dye of your color choice
  • Strong rubber bands
  • Spray bottle or squeeze bottle
  • Heavy rubber gloves
  • Clothing you'd like to dye

Step 1 Make sure you're wearing your gloves and you're located outside or in a well-ventilated area.
Step 2 Create a pattern by twisting or twirling your piece of clothing. Make sure you secure it using your rubber bands. Use as many rubber bands as you need.
Step 3 Fill your bottle with 1/2 bleach and 1/2 water. Make sure your balls of clothing are contained in a sink, bathtub or any big containers. Spray on a good amount of mixture from your bottle into the clothing. Let it sit for 8-10 minutes.
If you're using dye, read the instructions on the bottle and see how long to let the dye sit on the clothing.
Step 4 Remove the rubber bands (make sure you're still wearing your gloves). Wash the clothing using a mild detergent. Then let it air dry.
